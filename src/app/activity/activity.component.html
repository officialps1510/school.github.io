Co-curricular Activities

Personality development is the main objective of our education and can be achieved only by organizing different co-curricular activities. To develop this important aspect of education, we promote the students to take part in these activities by organizing them in the form of Inter-House Competitions. We also organize the summer-camp in which we develop different skills like music, dance, craft, calligraphy, yoga games etc. and provide free conveyance with other facilities at minimum charges.

Games Sport

Physical Education is very essential ingredient of our curriculum therefore we provide good facilities for different games like Table Tennis, Carom, Badminton, Basket-Ball, Volley-Ball, Yoga, Cricket, etc.
